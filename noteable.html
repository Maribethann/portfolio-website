Noteable

About The Project

What is Noteable?

Noteable is a note taking app designed to enable users to quickly capture their ideas effortlessly.

This was a mini project with a very tight 1 week deadline that was expected to be worked on alongside of another project. For this project I assumed full responsibility managing everything --from conception to the final deliverables.

The Challenge

A new client urgently requests 5 high fidelity wireframes for minimalistic note taking app to pitch to investors. The client has never worked with a designer before and has not prepared anything for the project. The goal is to quickly deliver the wireframes with focus on the simple and clean aesthetic.

Key Takeaways

What went well?

Overall I am very pleased with the outcome of Noteable; It was a valuable opportunity for me to challenge myself and put my design skills to the test.

Applying the lessons I've learned from previous projects like: seeking feedback from peers, utilising time management tools, & repurposing design components from previous projects, enabled me to streamline my workflow and create a beautiful app.

What can I improve on?

Through this project I learned how incredibly important the design processes like conducting research, low and mid fidelity sketches, user flows, user testing, and feedback rounds are to a viable product.

Due to the deadline, those processes just were not feasible. Though I do feel that the app would have greatly benefited from those missing design processes.

What's Next?

Ideally, the next step for Notable would be to get some user testing and see what works well and where the app has room for improvement.

Depending on the results of the testing it may be worth going back to the earlier stages in the design process to get better insights on what is important in a note taking app to better cater to my user base.

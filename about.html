About Me

My Story

Equal parts creative and empathetic, I am a passionate UI/UX designer dedicated to intuitive and beautiful user experiences.

I have a strong sense of empathy and an understanding of human psychology, thanks to my degree in healthcare administration and my experience working in customer service. By combining these qualities with a user-centric design philosophy, I work to create engaging interfaces that are not only visually compelling but also meet user's unique needs.

But I'm more than just a designer; my interests in digital illustration, gardening, and cooking fuel my creativity and inspire my design ideas. If you're looking for a motivated and cooperative UI/UX designer let's connect and talk about turning your vision into a reality.
